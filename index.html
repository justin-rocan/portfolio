Fangly

What started as a fun tech demo is now growing into a fully-fledged game. Play as a bipedal deep-sea fangly fish and roam the countryside to hunt down other Fanglers and steal their DNA. As your genes evolve, so do you. Spend your DNA on upgrades that allow you to customize your attack however you see fit. Fill the screen with bullets or maximize your damage with a single swift shot. The choice is yours.

Genre: First-Person Shooter
Platform: Web
Engine: Godot
Design: Minimal
State: Active

Wario Land 3 Successor

Proof of concept for a faithful Wario Land 3 successor that preserves what made WL3 the gem it is, while adding several much-needed quality of life improvements. Wario Land 3 is my favourite Game Boy game and, in my opinion, the best of the series. Over the years, there have been attempts to revive the series, as well as recent spiritual successors created by dedicated fans. While these are good games, they draw inspiration from my least favorite entry, Wario Land 4.

Genre: Platformer
Platform: PC
Engine: Godot
Design: Extensive
State: On-hold
