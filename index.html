Maximum Overslime

Avoid falling off the level by jumping around projectile slimes. The inability to move adds a layer of complexity to an otherwise simple game, as your only tool for escaping the edge is to jump into the enemy.

I had bigger plans for this game, including dozens of stages and several game modes, but I wasn't interested in over-optimizing it to fit within Pico-8â€™s constraints.

Plents

Have you ever wanted to grow a garden full of beautiful plants from all around the world? Have you ever wished those plants could walk, play, and love you back? The World Tree, Yggdrasil, is in danger from an unknown threat. Only the Plents, Yggdrasil's near-extinct guardians, can fight this blight. Garden your way to victory by raising your Plent friends from elemental sprouts, beyond biome-inspired seedlings, all the way to full-grown plants. How you raise your Plents affects how they evolve and what new abilities they'll learn. Then, take control of your Plents in a 3D platforming adventure, and bring home your spoils to help them grow.

At its core, this game is a love letter to the Sonic Adventure Chao Gardens. The Chao were a major part of my childhood and they hold a permanent place in my heart. At some point, I gave up hoping for the feature to return in new Sonic titles and decided to become the change I wanted to see in the world. The other half of the gameplay borrows inspiration from N64 collect-a-thons, especially Banjo-Kazooie. This project was started all the way back in 2013 and holds the record for my most revisited project.

Mark my words: If any game on this page gets finished, it's this one. I have vivid dreams about it almost once a month. It literally haunts me (in a lovingly caressing way).

Genre: Pet Sim / 3D Platformer
Players: 1

3D Map Engine

What started as a way to build exciting encounters for the D&D group I was running at the time, it developed into a full-fledged tool.
Battle maps could be created in the tool, complete with: dynamic lighting, layers, user-made 3D tokens, particle effects, directional audio, customizable grids.

My goal wasn't to replace VTT's like Roll20 and Foundry, but instead for it to be used by people playing tabletop game in-person with displays.
